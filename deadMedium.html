History


GeoCities was created by David Bohnett and John Rezner, originally under the name Beverly Hills Internet or BHI. It was originally launched in 1994, and the name was changed to GeoCities in 1995. Bohnett and Rezner created the free website hosting service in order to create online communities centered around particular interests and organize links to websites within these communities. The service was originally themed after neighborhoods in Los Angeles, hence the original name of Beverly Hills Internet. The initial intent of Beverly Hills Internet was just to serve as a directory, however soon after the launch of the company, they began to offer free web hosting services to users.
